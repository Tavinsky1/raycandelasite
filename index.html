Ray Candela is a cumbia and salsa band born in Berlin — where the grit of the city collides head-on with the heat of Latin American rhythm. We bring the dance floor alive whether it's a packed club in Neukölln or a sun-drenched festival stage.

Our sound draws from traditional Colombian cumbia and Caribbean salsa, reinterpreted through a Berlin lens: raw, direct, and impossible to stand still to.
